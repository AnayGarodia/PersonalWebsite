I am 18 right now, and like every 18 year old, I have decisions to make and stuff to figure out. I need to figure out what values I care about, what I want to hope to do with my life, why I have the beliefs that I carry, and most importantly, I need to figure out what I need to figure out.

The more I think about it, the more I realize that most of my decisions and beliefs have been a product of external circumstances and societal pressures rather than well reasoned, thought-out actions. I am writing to figure some of this out, and so that my future decisions are better thought out and more reasoned.
