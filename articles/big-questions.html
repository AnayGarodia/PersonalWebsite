Some Big Questions

2026-02

I am 18 right now, and like every 18 year old, I have decisions to make and stuff to figure out. I need to figure out what values I care about, what I want (hope) to do with my life, why I have the beliefs that I carry, and most importantly, I need to figure out what I need to figure out.

The more I think about it, the more I realize that most of my decisions and beliefs have been a product of external circumstances and societal acceptance rather than well reasoned, thought-out, actions. And this scares me.
